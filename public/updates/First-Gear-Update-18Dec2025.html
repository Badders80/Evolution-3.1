RACE PREVIEW

Otaki R6: The Work Is Done

First Gear heads into tomorrow’s race as the market favourite.

The preparation is complete, and First Gear arrives at Otaki tomorrow in prime order. The market has opened with him as the $4.00 favourite — a measured acknowledgement of the patience shown by the stable and the improvement he has displayed at home.

While the “favourite” tag is a confidence booster, races aren’t won on paper. Barrier 9 presents a genuine task in this field, requiring a clean beginning and the ability to find rhythm early. Having Bruno Queiroz aboard to navigate that challenge is a significant asset. The team has put in the hard yards to get him settled and focused, and tomorrow is about trusting that process.

CONDITIONS

Racing at Otaki is taking place on a Soft 6 track with the rail positioned out three metres. Although the course has received 22.5mm of rain over the past seven days, conditions are currently overcast with no rain recorded in the last 24 hours, which should help stabilise the footing for the runners.
